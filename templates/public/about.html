About Attendance Management System

A smart QR Code based attendance management solution designed to simplify attendance tracking for educational institutions.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a modern digital attendance environment where students, lecturers and administrators can manage attendance efficiently without manual paperwork.

Our Mission

We aim to provide a secure, fast and reliable QR-based attendance system that improves accuracy and saves time for academic institutions.

Why Choose Our System?

QR Code Attendance

Lecturers can generate QR codes and students can mark attendance instantly through scanning.

Real Time Tracking

Attendance records are updated immediately and available for monitoring.

Eligibility Monitoring

The system automatically calculates attendance percentage and student eligibility.

Role Based Access

Separate dashboards are available for Admin, Lecturer and Student users.
